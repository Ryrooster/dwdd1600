About the Author

  • Andy Weir is an American novelist known for his science fiction works.
  • He was born on June 16, 1972, in Davis, California.
  • Weir's debut novel, "The Martian," was published in 2011 and later adapted into a successful film starring Matt Damon.
  • He has a background in computer science and worked as a software engineer before becoming a full-time writer.
